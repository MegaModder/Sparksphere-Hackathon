About SparkSphere Hackathon

We're a team of high school students organizing an in-person hackathon for fellow high schoolers. Come be a part of an exciting day filled with coding and creativity! This hackathon is open to high school students, and we welcome participants of all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned coders. Best of all, the event is completely free! We'll also provide lunch and snacks to keep you fueled throughout the day. Just bring your laptop and charger! Don't miss this amazing chance to innovate and collaborate.
